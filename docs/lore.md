# Lore Foundations

Use this page to capture the fiction that fuels the game. Keep entries concise and actionable for writers and designers.

## World Snapshot

- **Era**: Post-calamity renaissance; old tech is resurfacing.
- **Tone**: Hopeful but wary; communities rebuild under lingering threats.
- **Magic/Tech**: Hybrid arcana and lost machinery, both unstable in untrained hands.

## Factions

- **Skybound Guild** — salvagers who chart floating ruins for relics.
- **Verdant Covenant** — protectors of living forests that encroach on old cities.
- **Iron Accord** — remnant engineers who hoard pre-collapse knowledge.

## Key Locations

- **The Hinge**: Central hub city where all trade converges.
- **Glasswilds**: A shimmering wasteland of fractured crystal storms.
- **Deep Loom**: Subterranean network that feeds power to dormant machines.

## Narrative Threads

- The truth behind the calamity and why relics are resonating again.
- Power struggles between factions over relic control.
- Personal stakes for the player’s origin and chosen allies.
